Top 5 CRM Software Companies

Published: March 26, 2024

Customer relationship management software is the heart of any growing business nowadays as all companies want to generate more leads, improve their lead-to-deal conversion rate, and provide excellent customer service.

There are currently dozens of top CRM companies that offer similar functionality, and many entrepreneurs wonder how to choose a CRM system tailored to the specific business needs and current challenges of a company.

For this reason, we created a list of the top 10 CRM software covering such aspects as:

  • General platform description
  • Key functionality blocks
  • Top well-known customers using the best CRM software
  • Pricing plan overview.

Salesforce

Founded in 1999 in California, the U.S., by Marc Benioff and Parker Harris, Salesforce is among the world’s leading cloud-based CRM systems with over 56,600 employees around the globe.
Since the first public release in 2004, Salesforce CRM has shown a tremendous 221.3x increase in revenue.

Below, you can see a chart of annual Salesforce CRM revenue growth from 2001 to 2021.

What Are the Salesforce CRM Features and Benefits?


Generally, as one of the top-rated CRM software solutions, Salesforce provides huge customization and integration functionality opportunities for dozens of business niches and models that exist in the market.

Below, we want to cover only the most essential and core features of Salesforce CRM you can get by using their product:

  • Contact management
  • Opportunity management
  • Quote management (quote to cash)
  • Powerful and insightful analytics functionality (Einstein Analytics)
  • Deep lead management
  • Advanced marketing features across all possible channels
  • Predictive and accurate sales forecasting
  • Top-notch security.

What Companies Use Salesforce?

Spotify, Amazon Web Services, U.S. Bank, Toyota, Macy’s, T-Mobile, Aldo, The New York Post, American Express, and Canon are among the global large enterprises, market leaders, and companies that use Salesforce CRM.

How Much Does Salesforce CRM Cost?

One of the top CRM companies provides 4 intuitive pricing plans charged per user based on the business model and needs you have.

Microsoft Dynamics CRM

Microsoft Dynamics CRM is one of the best CRM systems competing with Salesforce yet showing a 3-4% smaller market share.

The first product release was made in 2003 rolling Microsoft CRM 1.0 to the crowd. Since then, the platform has been renamed many times and finally became Microsoft 365 after the ‘October Update’ in 2018.

What Is the Key Microsoft Dynamics 365 Features List?

  • Powerful Business Central & Business Intelligence (BI) integration for visual insights into sales, marketing, and financial data in one place
  • Powerful integration with Outlook which allows you to leverage D365 benefits right from the Outlook application
  • Built-in Artificial Intelligence features powered by Microsoft Azure and Cortana
  • Cloud-first and mobile-first approach for instant and easy access to client data everywhere.

Who Are the Microsoft Dynamics 365 Clients?

Coca-Cola, BMW, HP, Chevron, Buyer, UPS, and Dr. Martens are among the world-known brands that trust Microsoft Dynamics 365 CRM – one of the top CRM systems – to meet their marketing, sales, and financial goals.

How Much Does Microsoft Dynamics 365 Cost?

Being one of the top-rated CRM software solutions, Microsoft Dynamics 365 provides on-demand pricing plans for any business challenges you may have, including sales, marketing, commerce, finance, supply chain, and many others.

For this reason, the pricing structure greatly differs from one segment to another, so take a look here to find more details on Microsoft Dynamics 365 pricing.

Zoho CRM

Zoho CRM is one of the top CRM systems released in 2005 by Sridhar Vembu and Tony Thomas in India.

With more than 360,000 customers worldwide and a large subscriber base that has recently exceeded60 mln users, the product has been showing an average annual growth of 15,1%.

What Are the Key Zoho CRM Features?

Let’s briefly overview the key functionality provided by Zoho CRM for your business:

  • Contact management
  • Lead management
  • Deal management
  • Daily workflow automation
  • Advanced sales process builder
  • Predictive sales and Intelligence
  • Broad customization opportunities
  • Powerful marketing automation tools.

What Companies Trust Zoho CRM?

Amazon India, Tassal, Suzuki, BOSE, and IIFL are the most famous brands using Zoho CRM on a daily basis to power their sales and marketing goals.

How Much Does Zoho CRM Cost?

One of the top CRM companies includes a 30-day free trial and starts at €57/user/month (if billed annually) and €69/user/month (if billed monthly).

What’s more, you can purchase a Zoho CRM Plus license which provides even more useful addons for your customer relationship management system.

Monday.com CRM

Monday.com CRM is among the top rated CRM software solutions with 1.05% market share and over $236 mln revenue in 2021.

The company was founded in 2012, and the product was first released in 2014 by Roy Mann, Eran Zinman, and Eran Kampf and is now valued at $2.7 billion by Bloomberg.

What Are the Key Features of Monday CRM?

  • Prospects, clients & deals management
  • Lead/Pipeline management
  • Wide contact database export capabilities
  • Powerful customization opportunities
  • Mobile-first optimization for high convenience
  • Insightful analytics and monitoring features
  • Visually appealing report functionality.

What Companies Trust Monday CRM?

Uber, Adobe, Hulu, Unilever, and Zippo are some of the companies that trust this top CRM software CRM and use their customer relationship management features daily.

What Is the Monday CRM Pricing?

One of the top CRM companies provides 4 transparent and affordable pricing plans: Individual, Standard, Pro, and Enterprise.

You can leverage the benefits of basic Monday CRM functionality by using an Individual plan which is absolutely free for up to 2 users.

SugarCRM

Launched in California, the U.S., in 2004 by Clint Oram, Jacob Taylor, and John Roberts, SugarCRM ranks high on the list of the best CRM systems and companies.

The platform has generated $72.8 mln revenue in 2020, and this number only tends to grow.

What Are the Key SugarCRM Features?

  • Support automaton
  • Sales automation and forecasting
  • Reporting and dashboard
  • Marketing lead management
  • SugarCRM mobile
  • Call center automation
  • Sugar Exchange
  • Cloud or on-premise deployment.

What Companies Trust SugarCRM?

Along with others, Mazars, Sennheiser, Postnord Logistics, Tetley, and Roxtec are using this top CRM software daily to empower their sales and marketing initiatives.

What Is Sugar CRM Pricing?

One of the top rated CRM software solutions provides 5 pricing plans:

  • Sugar Market (starting at £800 / month / 10K contacts / billed annually)
  • Sugar Sell (starting at £64 / user / month / 3 user min / billed annually)
  • Sugar Serve (starting at £64 / user / month / 3 user min / billed annually)
  • Sugar Enterprise (starting at £68 / user / month / 3 user min / billed annually)
  • Sugar Professional
    (starting at £41.60 / user / month / 3 user min / billed annually).
